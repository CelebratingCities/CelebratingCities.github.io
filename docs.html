About the Event

Uber and Deloitte are collaborating with the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) to help develop creative ladders of opportunity in the Baltimore - DC metropolitan area. Our mission is to spur creative solutions to last-mile problems and enable fast, safe, efficient, accessible and convenient transportation options that enhance the quality of life of the American people, today and into the future.
