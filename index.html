About

Celebrating Cities - Hack the Last Mile is a weekend hackathon where we'll design solutions that help the residents from the Baltimore and DC metropolitan areas affordably access their places of work, their schools of choice, job training opportunities, healthy food options, and other essential services from any neighborhood they choose to live. A panel of judges from Uber's engineering team are looking for the most innovative solution, and the winning team will have the opportunity to demo their application to Uber leadership here in DC.
